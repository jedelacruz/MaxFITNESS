Frequently Asked Questions

What are your gym hours?

Our facility is open Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Saturdays from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sundays from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Do you offer membership trials?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all new members. You can sign up for your free trial through our website or by visiting our facility.

What's included in a membership?

Our basic membership includes full access to the gym, all equipment, and free group classes. Premium memberships include personal training sessions, access to our recovery facilities, and more.

How do I cancel or modify my order?

If you need to cancel or modify your membership or any service, please contact our customer service team within 24 hours of placing your order or before your next billing cycle. You can reach us by phone or email.
